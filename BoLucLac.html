Bo Luc Lac (Shaking Beef)

Ingredients

  • 2lb Ribeye or filet mignon
  • 2 Tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon Maggi sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon sugar
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 Garlic cloves
  • 2 Tablespoon oil (cooking oil of your choice)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1 Bell pepper

Directions

Prep

  1. Combine the oyster sauce, fish sauce, maggi sauce, minced garlic, oil and sugar in a mixing bowl.
  2. Cut the beef into cubes and add the liquid mixture to marinate for a few hours or overnight.
  3. Chop the onions and bell peppers and combine in a seperate bowl.
  4. Slice the cucumber and cherry tomatoes (if desired) and set aside.

Cook

  1. Heat a pan/wok on high heat.
  2. Add beef and cook until close to desired doneness.
  3. Add onions and bell peppers and cook until both are desired doneness.
  4. Salt & pepper as needed.
  5. Serve the cooked beef with the cherry tomatoes and cucumbers with a bed of rice.
