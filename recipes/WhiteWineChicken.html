White Wine Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2-4 Chicken breasts
  • 1 Tablespoon butter (3 additional tablespoons set aside)
  • 1 Shallot (minced)
  • 4 Garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 Cup white wine
  • 1 Cup chicken stock
  • Parsley for garnish
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Pan sear chicken breasts.
  2. Using the same pan, add butter and shallots, Saute until translucent.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and garlic. Saute for another 2 minutes.
  4. Deglaze with white wine and reduce until it's very thick.
  5. Add chicken stock and reduce until thick.
  6. Add additional butter to thicken. Salt as needed.
  7. Add the seared chicken back and garnish with parsley to serve.
