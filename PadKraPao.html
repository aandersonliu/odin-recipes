Pad Kra Pao (Basil Chicken)

Ingredients

  • 2lb Ground chicken
  • 3 Tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 3 Tablespoon fish sauce
  • 3 Tablespoon Maggi sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon sugar
  • 1 Onion
  • 7 Garlic cloves
  • 8 Thai chilis
  • Handful of basil leaves
  • 2 Tablespoon oil (cooking oil of your choice)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Egg

Directions

Prep

  1. Combine the oyster sauce, fish sauce, maggi sauce and sugar in a mixing bowl.
  2. Chop the onion, thai chilis and garlic cloves and combine in a seperate bowl.

Cook

  1. Heat a pan/wok on high heat and add oil when hot.
  2. Add the onion, thai chilis and garlic cloves and cook until translucent.
  3. Add the ground chicken and cook until the outside is slightly cooked.
  4. Pour in the liquid mixture and continue cooking.
  5. Add in the basil leaves when the chicken is almost cooked.
  6. Salt & pepper as needed.
  7. Cook the egg sunny side.
  8. Add cooked chicken to rice and top with egg.
